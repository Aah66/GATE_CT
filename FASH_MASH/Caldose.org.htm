MASH3 (Male Adult meSH) and FASH3 (Female Adult meSH) are adult male and female voxel phantoms based on polygon mesh surfaces and anatomical reference data published by the International Commission on Radiological Protection. Both phantoms contain all organs and tissues necessary to calculate the effective dose. In addition MASH3 and FASH3 have skeletons segmented into cortical bone, spongiosa, medullary yellow bone marrow and cartilage, which allows for the application of mCT images of trabecular bone for skeletal dosimetry. A complete description of the phantom is given in: FASH_MASH_paper1.pdf , FASH_MASH_paper2  and in FASH_MASH_paper3.pdf . The voxel image files and the 3D images of the MASH3 and FASH3 are available as standing and supine versions. If you use the 3D images, please, quote this website as reference. Also, we can provide assistance for visualizing the phantom files if necessary.
